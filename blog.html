Discuss the scope of `var`, `let`, and `const`?

The scope of a var variable is functional scope and let,const variable is block scope.var can be updated and re-declared into the scope and let can be updated and can not be re-declared into the scope and const cantnot be updated or re-declared into the scope

Tell us the use cases of `null` and `undefined`?

1.You can assign the value `null` to a variable when you want to indicate that it intentionally has no value or that it shuld be cleared.
2.Variables that are declared but not assigned a value automatically have the value `undefined`.
